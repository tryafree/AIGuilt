Why Your Input Matters

Recent studies have shown varied public opinions on AI:

  • 15% see positive effects of AI on society, while 40% perceive negative effects[1].
  • Concerns range from job displacement to privacy issues and the spread of misinformation[1].
  • By 2030, AI is expected to be integrated into most aspects of life, potentially enhancing human capacities but also raising ethical concerns[2].

Your responses will contribute to a growing body of research on AI's societal impact and help shape discussions on responsible AI development.
