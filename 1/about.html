About AI Guilt

AI Guilt is a platform dedicated to exploring and addressing the complex ethical challenges that arise from the increasing integration of artificial intelligence in our personal and professional lives. Our mission is to provide a supportive community and valuable resources for individuals grappling with the moral implications of AI adoption.

Founded in 2023, AI Guilt emerged from a growing recognition that as AI technologies become more prevalent, many professionals across various fields are experiencing conflicting emotions about their use. These feelings can range from excitement about increased productivity to guilt over potential job displacement or concerns about the authenticity of AI-assisted work.

Our Goals

  • Foster open dialogue about the ethical implications of AI in various professions
  • Provide a safe space for individuals to share their experiences and concerns
  • Offer resources and support for navigating the complex landscape of AI ethics
  • Collaborate with experts to conduct and share research on AI guilt and related topics
  • Advocate for responsible AI development and implementation
